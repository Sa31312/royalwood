ABOUT US

About Our Company

We partner with accounting firms to provide a meaningful and smooth transition to founders and owners who wish to retire. We work intimately with firm owners to create a transition plan that is suited to each firm's unique client and employee situation. Through transition, our goal is to continue to deliver high levels of client service and employee satisfaction and retention. Beyond transition, we plan to create unmatched growth opportunities for our firms by providing the resources, technology and processes needed to better serve clients, employees, and communities.
